The Animation

This film has many similarities to the Spider-Verse movies. It has the same low framerate animations on characters to show the speed and power of the characters. The environment at times can look blurred out, which shows the speed and motion of the camera, without ruining immersion.

The Artstyle

While the artstyle seems very similar to the Spider-Verse movies, it's actually quite original. The artstyle resembles what seems like kid sketches or scetchbook artwork. When the characters are running across the rooftops, the moon looks lazily scribbled. Another shot shows a close-up of a characters face, and the eyes have scribbles on them. This is a fitting artstyle for a movie that deals with teenagers.
